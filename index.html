Our Story

Where Tradition
Meets Innovation

Founded in 2009, Chillox has been a culinary landmark in Dhaka, bringing the finest flavors of Asia to your table. Our master chefs, trained in the traditional kitchens of Tokyo, Bangkok, and Beijing, create dishes that honor centuries-old recipes while embracing modern culinary artistry.

Every ingredient is carefully sourced, every dish meticulously crafted. From the first bite to the last, we promise an unforgettable journey through the diverse and vibrant flavors of Asian cuisine.
